Vigilance Guidelines

The Vigilance Department plays a crucial role in ensuring ethical practices within the shipyard. Its key responsibilities include maintaining transparency, preventing corruption and fraud, and ensuring a safe working environment for all employees and stakeholders.

We encourage everyone to adhere to the highest ethical standards, and we provide guidelines for the prevention of fraud, corruption, and unethical behavior in our operations. If you encounter any issues, please feel free to report them through the grievance form below or directly contact our vigilance officers.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What happens after I report an issue?

Once you report an issue, our vigilance team will review it and take the necessary actions. We aim to resolve most issues within 7-10 business days.

2. Is my complaint confidential?

Yes, all complaints are handled confidentially. You can choose to remain anonymous if you prefer.

3. How can I contact the vigilance team directly?

You can contact the vigilance officers via email, as listed in the guidelines above.
