About Us

Shipyard Limited (GSL), established in 1957, is a leading shipyard under the Ministry of Defence, Government of India. Certified with ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, and ISO 45001:2018 by IRQS, GSL stands as a symbol of quality, safety, and environmental consciousness.

Our Strengths

  • Integrated Management System for Quality, Safety, and Environment.
  • World-class CAD/CAM Facility for design and simulation.
  • State-of-the-art Manufacturing Facilities.
  • Comprehensive ERP implementation for all functions.
  • Steel Preparation Shop for priming and cutting of plates.
  • Skilled workforce of over 1600 personnel and 200 engineers.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of services to our valued customers, including:

  • Designing and Building of Vessels
  • Repair and Modernization of Vessels
  • Technology Transfer
